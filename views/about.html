About Timnit Gebru

Timnit Gebru was born on Ethiopia but due to the unstable situation in Eritrea, she could seek asylum in the US where she completed most of her education.
She studied electrical engineering, worked for Apple and Google but is most known for her work in diversity and ethics in AI development.
Gebru worked for Google until December 2020 where she was let go after wanting to publish an article about the risks of large language models.
